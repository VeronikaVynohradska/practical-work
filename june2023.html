June 2023 Adventures

Reunited


Paris part

So a new name in this story - Dasha, her photos you can see in my travel gallery. A few words:

  1. 18th years old beautiful and talented person.
  2. were playing basketball in one team and each time lived together during Championship of Ukraine.
  3. really gifted photographer (I'm so proud to be her first model)
  4. currently live 3k kms away from me, in Lithuania.

As two extremely busy ladies we planned to meet on my 17-th birthday celebration, on 26th of February 2022. But because of one stupied motherfucker, it didn't happen.

Haven't seen each other for almost 2 years till this moment in the airport. Her dream was to see Paris, I could do it and did! It was challenging for me to be still awake, but we went exploring after the first second in the city.

Next 3 days were full of positive emotions and walking (each morning began at 8 and finally back home after midnight). Because of Dasha I got the Louvre, even more: liked that 3 hours there.

Ireland

As I live in Ireland wanted show the place and explore country more as alone I prefer to stay in Galway.

In 10 days in the country we went twice to Dublin, once to Athlone and to Limerick (I lost Dasha for like 10 minutes there, but it's definitely other story..)

One evening we had no plans and were kinda bored to stay in Galway, so I opened Google Maps and saw a title "National park" there, it was enough information to leave the next day. In final, it was a hiking and adventurous.

Only ten days, but:

  1. 5 oven pizzas
  2. 4 evenings in my favorite bar with live music
  3. 3 walks on the ocean coast
  4. 2 times in Dublin
  5. 1 climbing day
  6. feeding squirels

and of course tones of photo!
