July 2022 Adventures

To go to Paris just to pass exams...


The main issue that facing the Ministry of Education of Ukraine at the end of the school year was how to conduct final exams under the conditions of war?

So they decided to make one exam and select locations all over the world. The closest and the most convinient one for me was in Paris.

I went to French capital 2 days earlier my date "x" to be emotionally stable (spoiler: it didn't work as was expected) and do my best. My mum and younger sister were there for me.

During the free time we did a huge walking day and visited the most famous places (galleries even from inside).

And yeah, I succeded with quite high result (expected much lower one from History of Ukraine).
