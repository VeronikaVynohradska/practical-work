March 2022 Adventures

This month was the most sophisticated from the whole year, as I left home


From north-east to south-west

First part of our almost 2 weeks trip was the way from Kharkiv to Chernivtsi.

I left home with my family early morning and only late evening we reached Dnipro where stayed for 2 nights. It's important to mention that the man who took us and grannies (within next day) to first stop ended up transporting till the destination in Ukraine.

Next 2 days totally in a car, slept in Uman' for a few hours (this stop was neccessary for my youngers) and at night reached Chernivtsi.

Stayed there for a week, looking for normal flat to leave grannies in as it's too difficult in their age to travel from home to nowhere.

Chernivtsi-Suceava-Cluj Napoka-Vienna-Zurich-Paris-Tours-La Membrolle sur Choisille

Waved goodbye to loved ones and hello, Europe.

To say it was difficult equvals to say nothing. We had crossed entire Europe in 2 days. Sleeping on a cot inside Vienna's train station, changing train in Zurich in 5 minutes (sounds impossible, doesn't it?), shocked by condition of underground in Paris.. Litterally no sightseeing at all.

But we overcame all difficulties successfully and didn't even lose anyone!
