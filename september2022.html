September 2022 Adventures

My first Erasmus experience



In the beginning of September I received an amazing e-mail that I was selected to be a part of Ukrainian team in Erasmus Youth Exchange which took place in Sweden.

There were a lot of new stuff for me: from the first trip by plane alone to learning Lithuanian words on a project.

Stockholm

The trip started and ended in Stockholm and this city is definitely amazing, to be honest, I didn't want to leave and was searching for posibilities to move there.

This city is a combination of water and modern architecture. People are moving in different directions, but you see them as a floating river where each drop following the correct way.

A couple of unforgettable moments: night run under shower rain to couchsurfer's place (more about this you can read in my august 2023 adventures), found handsome second-hand shop, spent night in a hostel on a water styled as a real scandinavian ship and, of course, beautiful sunset and sunrise.


On a project

Me and 2 other Ukrainians arrived in the evening by train to a place located in kinda middle of nowhere!

Next 12 days we spent next to the lake, living in a big house all 50 participants from Ukraine, Lithuania, Turkey and Sweden together. I made really good friends with girls from Pribaltic country.


Spontaneous madness

I'm totally spontaneous person! With other project participant went to Gotheborg.

Now I'll also name this decision a stupied one, as came as a total wasting of money.

But still this city is the second largest in Sweden and totally different from Stockholm. It's more modern and has working/studying vibe. Maybe comparable with Kharkiv...

In total, the EYE is a perfect opportunity for easy-going, comunicable and extravert people!
