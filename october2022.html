Way to

We planned this trip 4 times and the 5th try was finallly successful, I bought a bus ticket 18 hours in advance, because had a few important things to do that day. In headphones lectures and me running from one part of the town to another.

My bus was at 7 a.m. from Paris, so no other choice than to go to French capital by evening train.

I spent the whole night in Paris.. Fantastic, isn't it? To try to sleep on a bench on a public bus's stop, as, there just trust me, Bercy bus station is a smelly ass hole!

After night without sleep staying awake to be presented on online studies in university was a nightmare, especially sitting in a comfy, warm bus. My advice: if it's possible, avoid day long-distance buses!


With Masha

I was exhausted, but very happy finally to see her in reality after 7 months in different countries.

We spent together 10 days, wanted to stay there more, as felt welcome and a part of Masha's family.


After Sweden it became a tradition for me to visit more than 1 city while travelling to any country. In Germany "Veronika was there" mark received such cities: Essen, Dortmund, Dusseldorf (haha, will remember forever) and Ketwig.
