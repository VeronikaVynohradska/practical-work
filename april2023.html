April 2023 Adventures

Adult's life

Made an important decision

This story is difficult to begin, because goes back to a childhood that wasn't as rosy as I'd like it to be.. Anyway, the past is unchangeable, but my future lays only on my shoulders!

I was waiting my 18th birthday as I can finally move whereever I want to. In my opinion, the best option was Ireland and my parents supported me in a way of buying single ticket + mum helped with all neccessary supplies (but still I left France without any medicine :)).

First few days

All my things fitted in a backpack and regular suitcase. Morning of 9th hugged my siblings last time (almost half a year ago), plane was late, but no nerves from my side. The moment I was waiting for years! I'm totally free!

After arrival to Dublin, I got my documents and waited for a while to get a temporary place. It was nice, even with a lot of refugees, but I spent there only 3 days and then bus took as to more permanent location.

Athlone

I was living in the former girls' school for 1,5 months. For me as a person who need loneliness and possibility to study it was severely. 300 people and only 3 fridges, horrible food, no wi-fi and A LOT of negative energy. I was ashame to see women drunk and dealing with problems kicking each other..

But the key point why this place not for me is lack of communication in English. There are 4 places in small Athlone where Ukrainians are placed, so everywhere even out from the school I heard mostly Russian and Ukrainian.


Ocean life

It was 31st of May when I moved to Galway, city located on a west coast. And currently I live there, like it so much and find sources to be happy every day.

This is MY HOME

P.S. maybe only for a year, who knows, at least this period..
