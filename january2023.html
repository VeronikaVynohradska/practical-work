January 2023 Adventures

Happy birthday, girrrrrl!

Way to

Taught by German trip and also understand the distance difference, collected money and bought plane ticket, but still had firstly to come to Paris (who would have thought the city of dreams would be just a stopover). The cheapest (remember: I'm still a poor student) airport Beauvais is located quite far from the capital and it takes 1,5 hours and 18 euros (almost the same price as a flight!) to get there.

I wasn't nervous because of my trip, but about no internet for 1,5-2 hours, so couldn't help my friend with studies.


With Lisa

Lisa and her mother (oh, such a nice woman, was teaching me geography till 10th form) were waiting for me and very happy to hug after almost a year far away from each other.

During my stay I had exams, but skillfully combined preparation to them with a survey of the proposed cities. So we visited all the north triangle: Gdansk (where Lisa's place is located), Sopot, Gdynya.

And after session went to spend 3 days in Warsaw. The key place visited there was "Polin". This is a museum dedicated to a jews' history. My impression was much higher than was expected, it worth each hryvnya from 300 paid! My high recommendation in case of ever come to Warsaw.

Ah, I almost forgot to mention: in Poland it is snowing sometimes and before coming I was recieving videos showing snow from Lisa. But in Warsaw it was a holly shit as the snow dropped down during the night and my trainers were wet in seconds outside. So instead of exploring, the last 7 hours in Polish capital we spent in cafe, not to catch a flu. Also I had a possibility to visit ice rinks in Sopot and Warsaw which I used.
